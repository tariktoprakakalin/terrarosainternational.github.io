Who We Are

We operate as a product-focused sourcing and trade intelligence network. Our role is to match the right product with the right buyer at the right time, based on verifiable information and operationally realistic procedures.

Product-First Approach

We prioritize technical specifications, logistics feasibility and financial verifiability—not the company name behind the product.

Independent Network

We work neutrally between refineries, producers, packaging facilities, logistics partners and end-buyers, building clear and fair trade bridges.

Dynamic Market

A product available today may be gone tomorrow. Therefore we don’t use static price sheets — we operate via LOI/inquiry-based evaluation.

What We Provide

Global Supply Matching

Verified producers and refineries for Jet A1, EN590, agricultural commodities, industrial inputs and more.

Procedure & Risk Filtering

We filter out unrealistic procedures and non-operational offers, focusing only on processes that match real logistics & banking workflows.

Strategic Advisory

For large-volume buyers: contract structuring, payment instruments, logistics scenarios and risk alignment.

Our Process

  1. Inquiry & Profile
    Buyer’s product, volume, destination port, timing and bank profile are clarified.
  2. Realistic Procedure
    We work only with processes aligned with real banking & operational workflows.
  3. Supply Matching
    Matching with verified refineries, producers or existing supply chains.
  4. Documentation & Execution
    SPA, procedures, inspection and logistics planning proceed with aligned parties.

Every transaction is evaluated through timing, credibility and risk. There is no “risk-free trade”, but unnecessary risk can always be avoided.

TerraRosa focuses on filtering unrealistic procedures early, saving time for both buyers and sellers.
